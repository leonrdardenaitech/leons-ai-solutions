Watz4 Dinner: The Intelligent Pantry Scanner

Overview & Instructions

Watz4 Dinner is an innovative, vision-based application that allows you to simply take a picture of your pantry or fridge, and instantly receive curated dinner suggestions based solely on the items scanned.

There is currently no other app on the market that can handle the pressure of generating high-quality recipes on the fly with such incredible ease of use. Simply point your camera, snap a photo, and let our custom AI architecture handle the complex meal planning constraints instantly.

How to Use:

  1. Launch the application from the portfolio.
  2. Use the camera interface to snap a clear photo of your available ingredients.
  3. Wait a few seconds while the AI vision models identify your ingredients.
  4. Review the dynamically generated recipe suggestions perfectly tailored to what you already own.
